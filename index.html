One of my favorite things to do in my free time is bike. I'm an avid mountain biker who is always trying to push my physical limits and get faster while keeping the style that I like to ride in. I never stop at a big gap jump or hesitate on a new trail because I trust the trail builders and hope for the best. Of course, that does not always go perfectly. Sometimes I come up short and worry about blowing out my rear tire, and other times I overshoot and land flat, bracing for the impact on my wrists and ankles.

My Bike

Another thing I like is cars. I enjoy looking at them, working on them, and driving them. Anything from a 1969 Ford Mustang Boss 429 to a brand new Koenigsegg Jesko catches my attention. The Mustang represents one of the most iconic muscle cars ever built, with its massive 429 cubic-inch engine and raw power, while the Jesko showcases modern engineering and extreme performance through its lightweight carbon fiber design and advanced aerodynamics. One of my favorite cars is the Aston Martin Vanquish Zagato Shooting Brake. It is not necessarily the fastest or most powerful car, but its design captures a unique balance between elegance and aggression. The hand-crafted bodywork and the signature Zagato double-bubble roof give it a style that few other cars can match.
